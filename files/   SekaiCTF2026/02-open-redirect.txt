<t>Open Redirect - Web</t>

This challenge had a login flow that redirected users based on an unvalidated "next" query parameter.

<st>Spotting it</st>

The login form passed a redirect target straight into a Location header.

<c>
curl -i "https://target.site/login?next=https://evil.example"
</c>

<st>Flag</st>

SEKAI{example_open_redirect_flag}
